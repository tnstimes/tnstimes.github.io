Uncovering quirks along TEL 4

The Thomson-East Coast Line Stage 4 (TEL 4) opened for passenger service on 23 June. While you may have started taking the train in your daily commute, have you noticed these hidden exits along TEL 4?

TE26: Marine Parade

At Marine Parade station, look closely at the directional signs in the unpaid area. Several stickers have been pasted on the signboard, covering the exit numbers "7" and "8".
Following the signages through the station, Exit 7 can be seen to lead to Marine Parade Community Club, while Exit 8 opens somewhere near Parkway Parade. According to floor plans by SMRT's retail arm Stellar Lifestyle posted on the East Coast Line construction blog, Exit 8 has a knock out panel for future development. (To be continued)
